Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Q-BitX Wallet ("the App") is a non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, uses, and shares.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Private keys – Your private keys are generated and stored exclusively on your device (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences). They never leave your device.
  • Personal information – We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Analytics or tracking – The App does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

  • Cryptographic key pairs (Dilithium/ML-DSA-65)
  • Wallet names and transaction history
  • Optional PIN hash
  • Custom RPC node URL (if configured)

All data is encrypted at rest using platform-provided encryption (iOS Keychain, Android EncryptedSharedPreferences).

3. Network Communication

The App communicates with:

  • Q-BitX blockchain node (default: qbitx.solopool.site) – to query balances, broadcast transactions, and sync transaction history. Only your public wallet address is sent; never your private key.
  • KlingEx API (api.klingex.io) – to fetch the current QBX/USDT exchange rate. No user data is transmitted.

You may configure a custom RPC node URL in the App settings.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not share any data with third parties. There are no ads, no analytics, and no data brokers.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete all wallet data at any time from within the App settings. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
